\section*{Procedure}

Materials:

\begin{itemize}
  \item two different balls
  \item one meter stick
\end{itemize}


\noindent When you're ready to start the experiment:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Drop the ball
  \item Stand back and watch how high the ball bounces
  \item Record the Data.
\end{enumerate}
